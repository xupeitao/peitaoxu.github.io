About Me

Hi there! I'm a Structural Engineer, GIS Analyst, and BIM Modeler passionate about driving innovation in urban and architectural design. My focus is on integrating GIS, data analytics, and BIM to create sustainable cities and buildings that respond to today’s environmental challenges.

I contributed to the National Air Pollution Surveillance (NAPS) Automation project at the Ministry of the Environment, Conservation, and Parks (MECP), where I automated critical data processing for air quality management. I also applied GIS expertise in Shanghai’s commercial district for XR advertising planning, performing data analysis and designing for sustainable urban projects: Signboard Lens in Urban Street.

I am driven by the potential of GIS and BIM to shape more resilient urban spaces and am committed to expanding my expertise to further sustainability initiatives.
